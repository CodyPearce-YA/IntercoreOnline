About

CRAFTMANSHIP

Today even some high-end speakers are assembled in a matter of hours, however, we work in a more meticulous way.

Our speed of working is set to the focus, the detail and the commitment that every craftsman and woman at Intercore brings to their job. Quality and perfection sets the pace at Intercore.

We are proud to say that Intercore speakers are both powerful and yet also sophisticated.

Expertise and craftsmanship. These are perhaps the expressions that best sum up the astonishing quality and finish of our speakers. When unpacking the speakers, it’s the impeccable German craftsmanship that inevitably catches the eye.

Every single one of our speakers is built by hand at ourstate-of-the-art head quarters and manufacturing facility at the home of Intercore in Berlin, Germany.
