    TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

  • Frequency range (-3 dB): 60 Hz - 40 kHz
  • Frequency range (-8 dB): 40 Hz - 40 kHz
  • Crossover frequency: 2200 Hz
  • Filter characteristic: 24 dB/Oktave Linkwitz-Characteristic
  • Nominal impedance: 3 Ohms
  • Recommended amplifier power: 30-300W
  • Sensitivity (2,83 V, 1 meter distance): 93 dB
  • Sensitivity (1 W, 1 meter distance): 90 dB

    • SPEAKER DRIVERS

    Bass / Mid-Range driver
  • Aluminum die-cast basket, extremely stiff, non-magnetic conducting — therefore no magnetic losses
  • Large-format rear venting of the basket to avoid flow losses within the chassis
  • High-rigidity anodized aluminum membrane
  • 68 mm Kapton voice coil, free from eddy current losses

    • J.E.T. Tweeter
  • J.E.T. Tweeter based on the Air Motion Transformer principle
  • Folded foil membrane with aluminum conductor parts
  • Rare earth magnets with high-energy density

    • FREQUENCY CROSSOVER

  • Mundorf foil coils for minimal loss in frequency range
  • Mundorf RXF capacitors with end-surface contacting, extremely low loss and high capacity
  • Metal oxide resistors with very low inductance
